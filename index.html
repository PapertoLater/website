The App

Why download it?

Usefull app for your newspaper! Boring to keep all of your papers to read later an article ? Hard to share with your familily or friend ? Found easily the article and save it on your Note app, Pocket, Instapper and other. You can also share it directly with your friend.

How to ?

When you'r reading your newspaper, open the app, select wich newspaper, and enter the title. You can use camera with the text recognization to go quicky. The App will open a webpage with the article and you can share it as you do with another stuff.

Get the app you want !

All the app is customizable. Your newspaper is not add by default ? Not a problem, you can add or delete your own newspaper. Furthermore, you can use the "other" research. Hide or Show newspaper you don't want by default. Switch beetween dark ou light mode easily!
